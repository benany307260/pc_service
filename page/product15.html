Product description

The Hodedah Storage Ottoman Bench is perfect for your foyer, sit here while taking your shoes off after a long day at work. Open up the seat and you will discover ample storage space for your hats, scarves, gloves and all your on the go items. This attractive ottoman features nail head trimming as well as a unique X-cross legs with center support. Place at the end of your bed to sit on or even use in front of your sofa to rest your legs. This versatile ottoman can be placed in any room plus its grey linen upholstery blends well with any room decor.
