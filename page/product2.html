Product description

The Starry Night is one of the best-known Vincent van Gogh paintings of all time. It depicts the view from the east-facing window of his asylum room at Saint-Rmy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an idealized village. Painted in June 1889 by the Dutch post-impressionist painter Vincent van Gogh. Since 1941 it sits proudly in the Museum of Modern Art in New York City.
