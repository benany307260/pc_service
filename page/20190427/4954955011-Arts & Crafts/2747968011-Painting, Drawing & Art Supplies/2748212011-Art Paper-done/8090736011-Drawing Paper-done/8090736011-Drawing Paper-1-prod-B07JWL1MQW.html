Product description

Bring your big ideas to life on ARTEZA'S 18x24 inch Drawing Pad. Experience the joy of this drawing pad not just once, but twice with the convenient two pack! Don't limit your imagination; Let ARTEZA help you keep creating.

Designed on heavyweight paper, this pad is incredibly durable and will stand the test of your creativity. This spiral-bound pad features micro-perforations that allow you to cleanly rip out true to size sheets upon the completion of your masterpiece.

Suitable for a wide variety of dry media, you'll find that this paper erases cleanly and blends smoothly without damage to the texture or fibers of the paper. Not only that, but the smooth texture of the paper offers the opportunity for you to be more in control of precise marks. Make the mark you want, not ones the texture of your paper dictates!
