Product Description

The Unique Multi-Media Art Pad

Turn Your Imagination into Beautiful Art

Theres more to art than writing, drawing, sketching or painting; its an expression of who we are, what we love, and what it means to be us. Thats why Bellofy Artist Pro Series Fine Art Drawing Paper is designed as a mixed-media paper that gives artists the ability to use a wide range of art forms and mediums to grow their creativity.

Product Details:

  • 100 Drawing Sheets (Heavy Weight)
  • Micro-Perforated Pages
  • Mixed Media White Paper for Sketching, Painting, Pencil, Ink
  • Top Coil Closure (Right and Left-Handed Artists)
  • Thick, Smooth Surface
  • Recycled, Acid Free
Discover Your Talents

This heavy drawing paper is durable, yet smooth, making it ideal for a wide range of artistic expressions, including ink and pencil drawing, watercolor, or even acrylic painting. And because its acid-free and absorbs ink more readily, you can worry less about smudges, stains, hand prints or color transfer.

Right and Left-Handed Artists

Unlike traditional spiral notebooks or sketchpads, our art paper pad features a flip-top spiral to make it equally usable by artists using their left or right hands. And the micro-perforated paper lets you draw, sketch or paint in the pad itself, remove a sheet to use, or just tear out a sheet and hang it on the fridge.

Beginners, Students and Professionals

Whether youre just starting out in art class, drawing doodles at home, or practicing on paper before you move to canvas, our art drawing paper is perfect for every level of artist. And because it offers a smooth, white texture, you can use a wide range of colors and drawing instruments on it to capture your art with precision.
